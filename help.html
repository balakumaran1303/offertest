If you have a confirmed Exchange Vacation that has to be changed or canceled, you will forfeit your exchange fee and possibly a portion of the Club Wyndham Points you exchanged. However, as an RCI member, you have the option to protect your exchange vacation by adding Points Protection. This protection will allow you to have 100% of your CLUB WYNDHAM® Points returned to your RCI account for future use.
Note: Points Protection is not insurance.

Points Protection allows you to cancel or change your vacation right up until the day of check-in, no questions asked.

If you cancel your vacation, you’ll be able to use your refunded Club Wyndham Points to book an alternate vacation. Without Points Protection, the standard cancelation policy would apply. Unless you cancel at least 120 days prior to check-in, you would only be refunded a portion of your Club Wyndham Points, depending upon how close to check-in you cancel.

To learn more about Points Protection, contact a travel guide Monday-Friday 8am-8pm (ET), Saturday-Sunday 8am-6pm (ET) at 1.800.572.0931.

Note: Exchange fees are not covered by Points Protection. Points Protection is not insurance.

Points Protection was designed to provide you with flexibility and peace of mind when booking your RCI exchange vacation. In simple terms, if you need to cancel your exchange vacation for ANY reason, Points Protection ensures that you will be refunded 100% of the Club Wyndham Points you used for the exchange.
