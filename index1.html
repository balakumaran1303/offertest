Booking Vacations

If you have a confirmed Exchange Vacation that has to be changed or canceled, you will forfeit your exchange fee and possibly a portion of the Club Wyndham Points you exchanged. However, as an RCI member, you have the option to protect your exchange vacation by adding Points Protection. This protection will allow you to have 100% of your CLUB WYNDHAM® Points returned to your RCI account for future use .
Note: Points Protection is not insurance.

Planning in advance is one of the best ways to get the vacation you want. The earlier you start to plan, the more vacation options you'll have to choose from AND the more likely you are to find what you’re looking for.

You should begin your search as early as possible. The earlier you search, the more exchange options you’re likely to find. RCI has a variety of search options to fit the needs of various types of vacation searchers.

  • Advanced Search: Allows you to select all of your desired criteria at one time and begin searching.
  • Favorites Search: If you generally search for the same type of vacation or resort each time, you can save your desired search criteria as a Favorite Search.
  • Ongoing Search: If you can’t find what you’re looking for in your initial search, RCI will continue to search for you, notifying you when a match is found.
  • Interactive Map: When you know the destination you have in mind and just need to find the perfect resort, the interactive map is a great tool to use.
  • Recommendations: Based on your search history and past travel, RCI will put together resorts matching your interests.
